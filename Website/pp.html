PRIVACY POLICY


Privacy and security of the personal data is our top priority. We want to assure you that your data is protected and safe with us. To understand how we use your data, please see more information below.


Personal Data Collection

There are a few reasons for why we might collect your personal data:

  • We may ask for your personal details, including your full name, home address, phone number, date of birth, and log in details.
  • In case we contact you, such as for the survey or promotions purposes, we may collect your feedback and any other contributions regarding our website and services, and may collect your personal data when contacted through the phone, email or social media channels.
  • Collecting information about your online shopping, to understand your preferences better.
  • Understanding what is used during the browse or shopping: website or mobile app.
  • Understanding shopping habits and preferences for future promotions.

How We Use It

  • Collected data allows us to manage your account with us.
  • To improve the services we provide and for better competition.
  • To fulfil your preference and interest in the products and services.
  • To provide a better and more secure place for your shopping experience.
  • Be able to provide the correct marketing to cater for your needs.

Sharing Your Data

We will only share your data with the stores who are the members of our website. This will allow them to provide you with better and more competitive sales and discounts.


How Your Data Is Protected:

We use a different set of tools to protect and keep your data safe, such as encryption, continuous monitoring for any system vulnerabilities, firewalls, cookies and strict access for all our employees.
